About Us
Created in 2019, Focus Business Consulting is a Virginia-based consulting company which delivers customer-focused solutions in a wide variety of fields, such as food, music, charitable giving, IT, and clothing/textile design. It also provides a high-quality credential validation service through a rigorous auditing process. It's primary focus surrounds a client's needs and goals, offering extensive product customization to tailor-fit solutions to the exact specification of a client's problems. It's relatively small size allows it to work alongside cutsomers for a truly individual and personalized experience.
